SCP-10663010516777777

Item Class: Apollyon

Object Name: “OLDER COUNTERPART”

Description: SCP-10663010516777777 is an anomalous visual entity capable of inducing persistent hallucinations upon exposure to its face. Affected individuals report seeing a red sky at night recurring within both dreams and waking moments. During these episodes, the subject perceives the same face appearing across reflections, darkness, and digital screens.

Containment Status: Apollyon. No method of containment is possible or effective at this time. Visual interaction with SCP-10663010516777777 imagery is strictly prohibited.
